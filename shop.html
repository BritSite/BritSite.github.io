Brit's Fox Shop

I sometimes feel crafty & make perler art, & if you want it, you can buy it!

But, what is perler art? Perler art are colored plastic beads that you place on a plastic board and then melt with an iron to make a cute design, character, or item.

I will soon be posting pictures of some of the perler art that I make. If you do not see one that you like or have an idea for what you want, you can also contact me to commission one for you!
Prices for each piece vary based on size, time, and materials. You can send payments for the Perler art through the paypal below or donate directly through the stream.
